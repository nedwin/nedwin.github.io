I co-founded [Great Question](https://greatquestion.co), a UX research platform used by teams at Canva, Notion, Intuit and a few hundred other companies. We raised a Series A in late 2025 and we're rebuilding the whole product for the AI-native era. It's the most fun I've had at work.

Before this I founded Elto, a marketplace for freelance web developers, which was [acquired by GoDaddy](https://techcrunch.com/2015/04/10/godaddy-acquires-elto-marketplace-for-web-pros/) in 2015. I write here about rejection, runway, and the long middle of building things.
