Now

Updated May 2026. Inspired by /now pages. The 2018 version is here.

Work

We closed a $13M Series A for Great Question in late 2025 and we’re rebuilding the product for the AI-native era — research workflows where the boring parts run themselves and the human stays in the seat for the parts that matter. This is the most fun I’ve had at work since the early days of Elto.

I’m spending most of my time on the rebuild, with our customers, and with the team. The rest is on growth: turning the Series A motion into a real go-to-market machine.

Family

Still married to Megan. Still in San Francisco. The plan to “stay a few more years” from 2018 has somehow turned into 12 years, which is roughly half a decade longer than we said. Melbourne calls a few times a year. Nieces and a nephew growing up too quickly.

Body

Running, slowly. The Presidio loop most weekdays. Working back up to a marathon (TBD which one).

Reading

Mostly nonfiction and the occasional novel. Currently on the pile: Slow Productivity, Working Backwards, and the new Sally Rooney. (I will report back.)

Writing

I’m trying to write more, here. The bar is “things that wouldn’t have a home anywhere else.” Topics I’m circling: what AI is actually doing to research, what it’s doing to founders, and how to manage psychological runway in year six of a company.
